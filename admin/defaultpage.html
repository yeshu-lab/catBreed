This is My Cat website content managment system (the administrative interface of the public website). The content enterd using this administrative interface will be reflected on the public interface (website).

The left pan holds menu of acttivities to be performed in this content management system.
  • To enter different types of cat breeds, click on Cat Breeds Menue
  • To enter different information category of cat breeds like (nutrition, health,grooming, etc.), click on Cat Breeds Info category.) menue
  • To enter the content Cat Breeds Information category which is under specific cat breed, click content menu.
  • To Restore Stock balance, click Stock Restore
  • To enter Customer information, Click Customer Data Entry
  • To Change aacount Information, Click User Profile
  • To enter customer feedback Click Feedback
